I'm a Java developer passionate about building high-performance Minecraft servers and Discord bots.

For the past years I've been deep in the world of Crystal PvP — designing custom plugins, optimizing servers for thousands of concurrent players, and reverse-engineering anti-cheat mechanics. From threaded Folia regions to packet-level protocol work, I love digging into the hard problems.

Beyond Minecraft, I write Discord bots, manage Linux infrastructure on Hetzner dedicated boxes, and contribute to the Hungarian gaming community.
